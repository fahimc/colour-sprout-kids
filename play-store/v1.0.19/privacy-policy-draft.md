# Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: 2026-07-11

Colour My World is made by Remetheia as a child-friendly offline colouring game.

## Information we collect

Colour My World does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not use accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, social login, or external user profiling.

## Local artwork and saved progress

The app can save colouring progress and exported artwork on the user's device. This information stays on the device and is not sent to Remetheia or any third party by the app.

Users can remove local data by deleting saved artwork, clearing the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstalling the app.

## Children

Colour My World is designed to be suitable for children. The app does not include ads, external tracking, in-app purchases, open chat, public sharing feeds, or account creation.

## Third-party services

The current app build does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
